This wasn’t made to impress you.
It was made because you matter.

In a noisy world full of temporary things,
you are my constant.

Loving you doesn’t feel dramatic —
it feels right.

If I ever stop and ask myself
what I’m grateful for,
the answer will always be you.

Always. Only you.
