For You

This isn’t something random I put together.
This is intentional. Just like you are to me.

I don’t believe love needs loud words or dramatic promises.
I believe in consistency. In choosing the same person, every day.

In a world that moves fast and forgets easily,
you are my pause. My calm. My certainty.

You don’t just make moments better —
you make life feel complete.

If I ever had to define what “home” means to me,
it wouldn’t be a place.
It would be you.

Always.
